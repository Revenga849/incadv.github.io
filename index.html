Incremental Adventure Prestige/Ascension calculator

Only works for layers in which you have zero points and zero stats upgrades (gameplay unlocks are fine)
Put layer you want to start calculating with in "Current Layer",
Layer you want to unlock in "Target Layer",
Amount of points you want in target layer in "Target Points".
Fill your current ascension unspent and exp upgrades below (0 if you have none).
We don't know how much level you can gain at each layer, so those combinations were loosely interpolated on available data.
Combinations of layer/level you can reach may differ.
But considering those selected, ascension point calculation is accurate.
